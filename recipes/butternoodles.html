Butter Noodles

Description

There is just something that is oh-so-satisfying about a bowl of buttered noodles. Add some garlic,
lemon juice, and parsley and you have one of the easiest and most flavorful noodle dishes that you could ever imagine.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles

  • 1.5 Sticks of butter

  • 2 teaspoons of minced garlic

  • 1/4 cup of lemon juice

  • 1 bunch of parsley chopped

Cooking Instructions

  1. Boil the noodles in a large pot

  2. Drain and leave 1/2 cup of water

  3. Cube the butter

  4. In a bowl: add the noodles, butter, garlic, lemon juice, and water

  5. Mix until butter is melted all the way

  6. Add Parsley

  7. Serve and enjoy!
