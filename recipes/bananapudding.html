Banana Pudding

Description

This quick and easy Banana Pudding just might be the perfect dessert! The no-bake treat requires only 5 ingredients and 15 minutes of prep for a cool, creamy, sweet treat that you can assemble in advance.
With Nilla wafers, Jello instant pudding, ripe bananas, and Cool Whip, this semi homemade banana pudding recipe is ideal for those days when you don't want to spend hours in the kitchen!
Keep reading below to find out what ingredients you need and how to make it!

Ingredients

  • Vanilla Wafers

  • Vanilla pudding mix

  • Milk

  • Bananas

  • Whipped Cream(Optional)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Whisk together pudding and milk in a large bowl

  2. Arrange vanilla wafers in a single layer in the bottom of a medium sized dish

  3. Spread the pudding over the wafers

  4. Top the pudding with one layer of wafers

  5. Refrigerate for at least four hours and serve!
