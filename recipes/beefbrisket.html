Beef Brisket

Description

To-die-for Beef Brisket that really makes the most out of this cut of beef! This Slow Cooker brisket is cooked in a simple homemade
BBQ sauce until itâ€™s deliciously tender and infused with incredible flavour. Serve with a side of Coleslaw or Macaroni Salad and steamed corn.
Or pile high onto rolls and make sliders!

Ingredients

  • Nice flavorful rub for brisket

  • BBQ Sauce or sauce of preference

  • Brisket in decent quantity

  • Slow Cooker

Cooking Instructions

  1. Rub brisket all over with seasoning rub and leave for 30 minutes

  2. Add sauce of choice and brisket into slow cooker and cook for 8 hours

  3. Remove brisket and pour liquid into a saucepan over medium high heat

  4. Bring to simmer and reduce heat until it thickens to a syrup consistency

  5. Drizzle brisket with oil then roast in oven at 350degrees farenheit for 15 minutes

  6. Remove and baste generously with sauce then return to oven for 5 minutes

  7. Repeat last step until it looks as you would like it

  8. Slice thinly across the grain and serve with remaining sauce. Enjoy!
